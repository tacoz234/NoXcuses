ðŸ“± iOS Notification Setup

For rest timer notifications to work on iPhone/iPad:

  1. Add to Home Screen: Tap the Share button in Safari, then "Add to Home Screen"
  2. Open from Home Screen: Always open the app from the home screen icon, not Safari
  3. Enable Notifications: Allow notifications when prompted
  4. Keep App Active: iOS may kill background processes if the app isn't used regularly
